West Bengal's cool Himalayan foothills are the joy of the state. Snowy mountain views, emerald-green tea estates, colourful Tibetan monasteries and charming colonial-era architecture can be found all across the foothills. While treks and mountain biking draw the active, you could just as easily spend a couple of days learning about Darjeeling tea while staying on a tea plantation. And when you are finished, Sikkim is just a stone's throw away!


Darjeeling

Darjeeling conjures visions of snow peaks, serenity of vibrant green hills steeped in splendour, a land of breathtaking beauty crowned by the majestic Himalayas.

Kalimpong

Kalimpong is a beautiful place of grate attraction. this sleepy little town situated at an altitude of 1,200 mtrs, some 50 kms to the east of Darjeeling.

Mirik

Mirik is a picturesque tourist spot nestled in the serene hills of Darjeeling district in West Bengal, India. The name Mirik comes from a Lepcha word meaning "place burnt by fire"

Sandakphu

Sandakphu is the highest peak in the state of West Bengal, India. It is the highest point of the Singalila Ridge in Darjeeling district on the West Bengal-Nepal border.

Lava

Surrounded by virgin pine forests and often hidden in mists and clouds, this small village lays few away from Kalimpong on the old trade route to Bhutan.

Lolegaon

A small peaceful hamlet in Kalimpong, Lolegaon is nature's paradise on its own with beautiful landscape, comprising lush green forest and serene valleys.
