West Bengal's cool Himalayan foothills are the joy of the state. Snowy mountain views, emerald-green tea estates, colourful Tibetan monasteries and charming colonial-era architecture can be found all across the foothills. While treks and mountain biking draw the active, you could just as easily spend a couple of days learning about Darjeeling tea while staying on a tea plantation. And when you are finished, Sikkim is just a stone's throw away!


Digha

The Digha beach is popular since it happens to be one of the widest sea beaches all across the world. Digha beach is providing the best sunset view in the entire Bengal.

Mandarmani

Mandarmani is a seaside resort village in East Medinipur district and at the northern end of the Bay of Bengal. It is one of the large and fast developing of West Bengal.

Tajpur

Located 16 kms away from Digha, Tajpur till date remains one of the most unknown beaches in India. Tajpur offers a sea beach lined with Tamarisk trees as its prime attraction
